Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our premium transportation services.

How do I book a ride?

The easiest way is through WhatsApp - simply send us your pickup location, destination, date, and time. We'll provide an instant quote and confirm your booking within minutes.

What vehicles do you offer?

We have a premium Mercedes fleet including V-Class (up to 7 passengers) and E-Class (up to 4 passengers). All vehicles feature leather interiors, climate control, free WiFi, and premium amenities.

Are you available 24/7?

Yes! We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. Whether you have an early morning flight or a late-night arrival, we're here to serve you.

Do you monitor flights?

Absolutely! We track all flights in real-time and adjust pickup times accordingly. If your flight is early or delayed, we'll be there when you land - no extra charge for flight delays.
